Our Story

From a five-person consultancy to a global delivery team

TechNova Solutions began in 2016 with a simple frustration: too many enterprise software projects shipped late, over budget, and disconnected from the business problems they were meant to solve.

What started as a five-person team building internal tools for a single manufacturing client has grown into a 500-person global organization spanning cloud engineering, AI, security and design — without losing the founders' original obsession with outcomes over output.

Today we serve 500+ clients across 35 countries, but the standard hasn't changed: senior engineers, transparent delivery, and software that earns its keep.
